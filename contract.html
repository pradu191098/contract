Megapolis Township

– 1/2/3 BHK Flats in Hinjawadi IT Park

Megapolis – Integrated Township in Hinjawadi offers Smart and Premium 1/2/3 BHK
flats in Hinjawadi. Likewise, homebuyers can choose from Under Construction or
Ready Possession Flats in Hinjawadi IT Park. The Township was conceived so that the
rising number of computer software professionals working in the IT Park would not
have to commute for long distances.
Megapolis was established in 2008 as an integrated township for IT professionals. It
is a joint venture between Kumar Properties and ABIL. Consequently, it aims to con
struct contemporary townships.

1, 2, 2.5, 3 BHK Flats in Hinjawadi IT Park

living in township.
